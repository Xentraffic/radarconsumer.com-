What Is Keto?

Keto, also known as Ketosis, is a process in which your body naturally enters a state where it will burn fat instead of muscle.

A popular benefit to this is that you can eat whatever you like without worrying about calories, fat, or carbs since your body will continue to utilize fat for energy – which is comprised of carbs and sugars.
